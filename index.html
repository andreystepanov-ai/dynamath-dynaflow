Dyna Flow Visualisation

This demo illustrates the core idea behind the dynamic mathematics formalism: concepts, relations and processes flow continuously and interact through a field. The animation below simulates a fluid flow field; each particle represents a concept moving through the semantic landscape. The patterns that emerge symbolise the structures described by the equations in the paper.

Fundamental dynamic equations used in the paper (for example, the Maxwell-like field equations) establish that changes in one field induce changes in another. Here, the velocity field \(\mathbf{v}(x,y)\) is defined by a set of simple sine and cosine functions, and particles follow this field, drawing out trajectories reminiscent of the dynamic flows of ideas.
